Paint Protection

Premium 3M steam wash and ceramic coating for lasting shine

About This Service

Protect your vehicle's paint with our advanced ceramic coating and 3M steam wash services. Ceramic coating creates a durable, hydrophobic layer that repels water, dirt, and contaminants while enhancing gloss. Our 3M steam wash provides deep cleaning without harsh chemicals, preserving your paint's integrity.

Our Process

1. Deep Clean

3M steam wash to remove all contaminants.

2. Paint Correction

Polish to remove swirls and scratches.

3. Coating Application

Apply ceramic coating in controlled environment.

4. Curing

Allow coating to cure for maximum durability.

Why It's Important

Paint protection extends the life of your vehicle's finish and maintains its appearance for years. It provides superior protection against UV rays, bird droppings, tree sap, and minor scratches. This investment preserves your vehicle's value.

Prevention & Maintenance

After ceramic coating, wash regularly with pH-neutral soap. Avoid automatic car washes with harsh brushes. The coating lasts 2-5 years depending on maintenance. Reapply as needed to maintain protection.
