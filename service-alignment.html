Precision alignment for smooth handling and extended tyre life

About This Service

Proper wheel alignment ensures your vehicle drives straight, handles predictably, and maximizes tyre life. We use computerized alignment equipment to adjust camber, caster, and toe to manufacturer specifications for all vehicle types.

Our Process

1. Pre-Alignment Check

Inspect suspension and steering components.

2. Measurement

Computer-aided measurement of current alignment.

3. Adjustment

Precise adjustment to factory specifications.

4. Road Test

Test drive to confirm proper tracking.

Why It's Important

Misalignment causes uneven tyre wear, poor handling, and reduced fuel efficiency. It can also strain suspension components. Regular alignment saves money on tyres and improves driving safety and comfort.

Prevention & Maintenance

Get alignment checked after hitting potholes or curbs. Have it done annually or every 20,000 km. Watch for pulling to one side or uneven tyre wear. Avoid overloading your vehicle.
