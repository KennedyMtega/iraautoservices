About This Service

Restore your vehicle's appearance with our professional painting services. Available exclusively at our Mbezi Branch, we offer complete paint jobs, panel resprays, and color matching. Our paint booth ensures a factory-quality finish that protects and beautifies your vehicle.

Our Process

1. Assessment

Inspect damage and color match requirements.

2. Preparation

Sanding, filling, and priming surfaces.

3. Painting

Multiple coats in controlled paint booth.

4. Finishing

Clear coat application and polishing.

Why It's Important

Paint protects your vehicle's body from rust and corrosion. Damaged paint exposes metal to the elements, leading to expensive rust repairs. A good paint job also maintains your vehicle's resale value.

Prevention & Maintenance

Wash your car regularly to remove corrosive dirt and salt. Wax every few months for added protection. Park in shade when possible. Address chips and scratches promptly before rust starts.
