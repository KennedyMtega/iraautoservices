Frequently Asked Questions

What services does IRA Auto Services offer?
IRA Auto Services specialises in providing automotive maintenance and spare part solutions. Our services include: Wheel Alignment, Tyre Sales & Installation, Brake Pad Services, Oil Changes, and Car Painting (available only at Mbezi Branch). We also offer a wide range of genuine spare parts covering everything from brake systems and suspension components to engine and body parts.
Where are IRA Auto Services branches located?
IRA Auto Services operates three branches: Mbezi Branch (Plot 85, Jogoo area, Mbezi Beach, Kinondoni, Dar Es Salaam), Sinza Branch (Sinza, Dar Es Salaam), and Namanga Branch (Namanga, Tanzania). All branches provide automotive maintenance and spare part solutions.
How do I contact IRA Auto Services?
You can contact us by calling +255 752 008 008 or +255 784 292 222, sending us a WhatsApp message, or emailing sales@ira.co.tz. We'll respond promptly and provide all necessary details.
What are your working hours?
Our working hours are: Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Saturday: 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM, Sunday: Closed. We are closed on public holidays.
Where is car painting available?
Car painting services are available only at our Mbezi Branch. Our other branches (Sinza and Namanga) offer all other services including wheel alignment, tyre sales & installation, brake pad services, and oil changes.
Do you sell spare parts?
Yes, we offer a wide range of genuine spare parts covering everything from brake systems and suspension components to engine and body parts. All our spare parts are genuine and sourced from trusted manufacturers to ensure quality and reliability.
What makes IRA Auto Services different?
IRA Auto Services is a subsidiary of Kisangani General Enterprise, which has over 40 years of excellence in the automotive sector. Our legacy is built on experience, quality, strong testimonials and a deep understanding of what vehicle owners need. We combine decades of expertise with innovative solutions to deliver superior car care and spare part services.
